CompanyRide is an application that helps people to share their everyday rides. Massive car sharing will reduce trafic conjestion and improve our health and environment.

CompanyRide key features:

Smart matching algorithm
Matching algorithm releaces people from exhausing searches.
Free of contact information sharing
All ride relevant communication is done within application.
Ride management tools
Support of a ride after a matching has occured. Ride is presented and managed as a calendar event.
